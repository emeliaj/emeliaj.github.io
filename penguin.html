Across the Antarctic Peninsula, penguin colonies are being devastated by the effects of climate change. When it warms, it rains. When it rains, penguin chicks die. You can help by insulating your house, reusing and reducing items, carpooling, etc. You can save penguin chicks from a shivery death.
